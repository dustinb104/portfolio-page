Team Management App

Our app will allow intramural leagues, youth leagues, and school leagues to register team members and organize team schedules and events. The inspiration of this app came from a team member, Grant Dalgo, and the inspiration was to build a platform that would make it easier for leagues to manage teams and schedules. The front end of this application will display a general overview of the team, their stats, and their upcoming events. And our database will store the information of team members, team stats, and team schedules. The hope for this app is to provide an easier way for teams to connect and schedule games within leagues so that there is less time being spent scheduling games and more time can be used to coach team members and playing the game. My team members for this project are Nick Hall and Grant Dalgo.
